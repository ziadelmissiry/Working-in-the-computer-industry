Privacy Issues

What type of data is collected?

  1. Emails
  2. Questionnaires this includes name, address, etc
  3. Date and time of visit

How long can a company keep your data?

Well, it depends on the country you're in. In the UK a company can hold your data for up to 12 months. Unless, the law requires them to hold on to it for more.

Who gets to see the data?

Companies are not allowed to disclose someone’s private data. They are required by the law to keep it safe. Some companies like to use third parties to process the data and if they wish to do so these third parties are required to keep the data safe as well.

What are your rights?

You have the right to ask a company or organisation to delete all your data. You also have the right to amend your data and the right to refuse your data being processed.

What is the amount of remote attacks that businesses face?

53% of businesses have seen an increase in email phishing attacks during the pandemic with a third saying these phishing attacks have been more successful. On average, organisations are remediating 1185 phishing attacks each month, with an average of 40 per day
